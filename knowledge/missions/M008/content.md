# Content Strategy

**Content Pillar:** "AI Business Blueprint" series — positioning this channel as the go-to resource for data-backed, executable AI business models in India (not hype, not theory).

**Format:** Long-form YouTube video (10-14 minutes) — optimized for search + suggested feed, later repurposed into 3-4 Shorts/Reels for top-of-funnel reach.

**Target Audience:** Indian audience aged 20-40 — side-hustlers, students, freelancers, small agency owners, and content creators looking for a realistic, numbers-backed path to ₹1 Lakh/month income using AI.

**Positioning Angle:** "Here's the exact business model — with real market numbers — that can get you to ₹1 Lakh/month using AI content in 4-6 months." Credibility is built through specific data points (market size, CAGR, revenue splits) rather than generic motivation.

**Funnel Strategy:**
- Video builds authority → drives to free newsletter/community (owned audience asset)
- Newsletter later upsells to a paid course/cohort or the AI Content-as-a-Service agency itself
- Comments section used to identify people wanting to become B2B clients or team members
