# Content Strategy

**Core Angle:** Position this as a "business blueprint" video — not hype, not theory, but a data-backed, step-by-step opportunity breakdown for someone who wants to start an AI-powered media/content business in 2024-2025.

**Target Viewer:** Aspiring solopreneurs, side-hustlers, freelancers, marketing professionals, and small agency owners in India (and globally) who are AI-curious but don't know how to convert AI tools into actual revenue.

**Content Pillar:** "Opportunity + Proof + Playbook" — establish market size credibility first, then show the exact business model, then give a realistic income roadmap (₹1 Lakh/month) so the viewer leaves with both belief and an action plan.

**Format:** Mid-length (10-14 min) YouTube video — talking head + screen-share style graphics/data overlays. Positioned as evergreen, searchable "business idea" content that ranks well long-term.
