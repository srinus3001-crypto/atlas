# Content Strategy

**Format:** Educational/Opportunity Breakdown (Talking Head + B-Roll + Screen Recordings)
**Niche:** AI Business Opportunities / Passive Income / Creator Economy (India-focused, global appeal)
**Target Platform:** YouTube (Primary), repurposed to Shorts/Reels/Instagram
**Content Pillar:** "Data-backed business opportunity breakdowns" — positions channel as a trusted research-driven authority, not hype-driven "get rich quick" content
**Audience:** Indian professionals/students (18-34) + global English-speaking side-hustle audience
**Differentiation Angle:** Unlike generic "AI will make you rich" content, this video leads with market data, real numbers, and a phased execution roadmap — building credibility through evidence, not exaggeration
**Video Goal:** Drive watch time + position channel/service as the go-to resource for AI Media business building (funnel toward SME service offering or paid community)
