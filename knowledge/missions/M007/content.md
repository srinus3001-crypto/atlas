**Target Audience:** Indian side-hustlers, aspiring creators, and digitally-savvy professionals (22-35) searching for realistic AI income models — not overnight-riches seekers.

**Content Pillar:** Educational + Strategic Breakdown ("Business Teardown" genre) — builds authority and trust, positions Atlas as the go-to source for AI-enterprise playbooks.

**Differentiator:** Most "AI YouTube automation" content is generic and Western-facing. This script leans into the underserved regional-language + Indian market angle — a specific, defensible moat.
