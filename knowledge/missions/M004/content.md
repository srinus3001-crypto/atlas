# Content Strategy

**Core Positioning:** Position this as a *transparent, data-backed business build* — not another "get rich quick" creator video. The angle: use Atlas Enterprise's own research to show a real, numbers-driven roadmap to ₹1 Lakh/month using AI-augmented content — credibility through specificity, not hype.

**Format:** Long-form YouTube (10–14 min), Hinglish delivery, screen-recorded data/graphics + talking-head segments for trust-building. This becomes Episode 1 of a series ("Build With Atlas") — each subsequent video documents progress, creating a binge-worthy build-in-public arc.

**Content Pillars for the Channel (from research):**
1. AI tool breakdowns for career/productivity (high search intent, low competition in Hindi)
2. Case-study format: "I tried X AI tool to make money — here's what happened"
3. Personal finance + side-income explainers
4. Behind-the-scenes: building this exact business (meta-content = audience retention engine)

**Distribution Loop:** Shorts (hook clips from this video) → YouTube long-form → Email/newsletter capture (lead magnet: free "AI Income Blueprint" PDF) → affiliate + digital product upsell.
