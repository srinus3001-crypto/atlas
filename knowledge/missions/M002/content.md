# Content Strategy

**Vertical:** AI/Tech explainers for career upskilling (highest ROI niche per research — dual audience access to Indian + global CPM markets, low production cost via AI tools, evergreen search value).

**Format:** Faceless YouTube long-form (8-10 min) + repurposed into 8-10 Shorts/Reels per video.

**Cadence:** 2-3 long-form videos/week + daily shorts (matches the 15-20 pieces/week target from research).

**Monetization Path:** Ad revenue (once monetized) → affiliate links (AI tools mentioned in video) → sponsorships (post 20-50K subs) → digital product (prompt pack/template) sold via newsletter link in description.

**Differentiator:** Quality-controlled AI production — script every video for a real practical takeaway (not generic AI fluff), per "quality before speed" principle.
